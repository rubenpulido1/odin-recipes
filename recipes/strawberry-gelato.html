Strawberry Gelato

Description

This is a strawberry gelato recipe that can be made in under a day and yields 8 servings.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of ripe strawberries, hulled and halved
  • 2 tablespoons and 2 teaspoons of white sugar
  • 1/2 cup of water
  • 1/4 teaspoon lemon juice
  • 1/8 teaspoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1/2 a tiny pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Blend all ingredients.
  2. Add the blend to a thin pan sheet that is covered and place it in the freezer for 20 min.
  3. Every 20 minutes mix the blend with a fork and repeat for 2-3 hours.
